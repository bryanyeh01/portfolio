Pintle Injector Design

Design and documentation of a liquid rocket pintle injector assembly focused on manufacturable geometry and clear engineering communication.

What This Project Is

This project focuses on designing a pintle injector that is both functional and manufacturable. The goal is to create a design that can realistically be built and clearly communicated through engineering drawings.

CAD model of full injector assembly

What I Worked On

  • CAD modeling of injector geometry
  • Engineering drawings
  • Design revisions based on feedback
Engineering drawing for manufacturable plate

Where I'm At

The design is still being refined, but has reached a stage where geometry is validated and physical prototypes can be tested.
