FSAE Brake Rotor FEA

Thermal and structural validation of a Formula SAE brake rotor using ANSYS. The goal was to understand temperature distribution, stress behavior, and safety margins under braking loads.

What This Project Is

This project focuses on analyzing a brake rotor under realistic braking conditions. The simulation combines thermal loading from friction with structural analysis to evaluate stress and deformation.

Brake rotor CAD model

What I Worked On

  • Thermal simulation setup using heat input from braking
  • Structural analysis using imported temperature fields
  • Boundary condition setup for realistic constraints
  • Safety factor evaluation

Results

The simulation showed stress concentrations near mounting points and highlighted areas where the design may fail under extreme conditions.
